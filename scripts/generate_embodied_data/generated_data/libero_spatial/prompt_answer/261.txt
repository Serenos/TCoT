**Overview of the Task**

The plate is also on the table, but at a different location. The goal is to navigate the robotic arm to the black bowl, grasp it, lift it, and then move it to the plate and release it.

   - **Justification**: The robot needs to approach the black bowl to be able to pick it up. The bowl is on the cookie box, so the robot must move forward to reach it.

   - **Interval**: Steps 25-43
   - **Justification**: Once the robot is aligned with the bowl, it needs to move down to grasp it. This ensures that the gripper can securely hold the bowl without knocking it over.

4. **Lift the black bowl**
   - **Interval**: Steps 57-63
   - **Justification**: After grasping the bowl, the robot needs to lift it to clear the cookie box and any potential obstacles. This ensures that the bowl can be moved safely.

5. **Move towards the plate**
   - **Interval**: Steps 66-72
   - **Justification**: With the bowl in hand, the robot needs to navigate to the plate. Moving left is necessary to align with the plate's position.

6. **Move down to place the black bowl on the plate**
   - **Interval**: Steps 76-80
   - **Justification**: Once aligned with the plate, the robot needs to move down to place the bowl on it. This ensures that the bowl is placed correctly and securely.

7. **Stop after placing the bowl**
   - **Interval**: Steps 81-96
   - **Justification**: After placing the bowl, the robot needs to stop to complete the task. This ensures that the bowl is stable and the task is successfully completed.
