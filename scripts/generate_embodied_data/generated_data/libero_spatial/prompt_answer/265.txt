   - **Justification**: The white plate is located to the left of the black bowl. The arm must move left to align itself with the plate before placing the bowl on it.

   - **Justification**: Once the arm is aligned with the white plate, it needs to stop to ensure a stable position before releasing the bowl onto the plate.
