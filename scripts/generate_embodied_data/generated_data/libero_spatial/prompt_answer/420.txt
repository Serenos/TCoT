   - **Justification**: The black bowl is the target object that needs to be picked up. The robotic arm must first move to its location to grasp it. This involves moving left to align with the bowl's position.

   - **Justification**: Once the arm is aligned with the bowl, it must lower itself to the appropriate height to grasp the bowl. This ensures that the gripper can securely hold the bowl without knocking it over or missing it.
   
3. **Lift the Bowl** (Steps 68-77)
   - **Justification**: After grasping the bowl, the arm must lift it to clear any obstacles and prepare for the movement to the plate. Lifting the bowl also helps in maintaining a stable grip and preventing it from slipping.
   
4. **Move to the Plate** (Steps 79-89)
   - **Justification**: With the bowl lifted, the arm must now move forward to reach the plate. This involves navigating the arm to the plate's location while ensuring the bowl remains securely held.
   
5. **Place the Bowl on the Plate** (Step 89)
   - **Justification**: Once the arm is aligned with the plate, it must lower the bowl gently onto the plate. This completes the task by placing the bowl in the desired location.
